Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my Binance or Coinbase account?

You can connect your exchange accounts through the app's settings. Navigate to the Binance or Coinbase section in the app menu, and follow the instructions to securely connect your API keys.

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store. You can view, modify, or cancel your subscription in your Apple account settings.

What's your response time for support requests?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday-Friday).

Do you offer refunds?

Refund requests are handled directly by Apple through the App Store. Please contact Apple Support for refund inquiries.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We welcome feature suggestions and feedback. Please use the contact form above to share your ideas.

How do I report a bug?

Please use the contact form above and include as much detail as possible, including your device model, macOS version, app version, and steps to reproduce the issue.

Is my exchange API key secure?

Yes, your API keys are encrypted and stored securely on your device. We never transmit your API keys to our servers. All exchange connections are made directly from your device to the exchange APIs.

What features are available in the free version?

The free version includes basic chart viewing, limited symbol tracking, AI Chat access, and basic portfolio features. Premium features include unlimited charts, full portfolio tracking, exchange integrations, heatmaps, screeners, and more.
